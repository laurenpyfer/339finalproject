About Page

I'm from Mill Valley, California, a small town just north of San Francisco. I grew up spending most of my time in nature whether it was hiking to the beach, exploring the redwoods, or searching through tidepools. My two brothers are the best friends I could ever ask for. Charlie is 20 and a freshman playing lacrosse and football at the USAFA. Maxwell is 16 and spends most of his time with his girlfriend or playing basketball. Our mom and dad are the hardest working people I know and somehow still manage to swim marathons and climb the highest peaks. The old guy in the family is Donner, a perfect 14 year old rescue.
